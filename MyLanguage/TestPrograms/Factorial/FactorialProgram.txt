Guide how to build factorial:

	Required degree.

	current_answer = 1.

	current_degree = 1.

	See taking_factorial.

	Write current_answer.

End of instruction.

Instruction of taking_factorial:

	If (degree <= current_degree)

		List of commands:

		current_degree = current_degree + 1.

		current_answer = current_degree * current_answer.

		See taking_factorial.

		End of commands.

End of instruction.
